## Idea 1: "Editorial Minimalism" — Inspired by Cereal Magazine / Kinfolk

**Design Movement:** Scandinavian Editorial Minimalism meets luxury hospitality branding

**Core Principles:**
1. Extreme restraint — every element earns its place
2. Typography as architecture — type does the heavy lifting
3. Negative space as luxury signal — emptiness conveys exclusivity
4. Muted warmth — cream, stone, charcoal with whispers of gold

**Color Philosophy:** A palette of warm neutrals that evokes handmade paper, aged linen, and brushed brass. The cream (#FAF7F2) background feels like a premium stationery stock. Charcoal (#2C2C2C) anchors text. Muted gold (#C9A96E) appears only as a reward — on selection states and progress indicators — making it feel earned.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-viewport single-question screens with generous vertical centering. The 2-column image grid floats in the upper-middle zone, with navigation anchored at the bottom. No sidebar, no header clutter — just the question and the options.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Thin horizontal gold rule as a recurring motif (welcome divider, progress bar)
2. Image cards with cinematic dark overlays and bottom-aligned white type
3. Pill-shaped navigation buttons with uppercase micro-tracking

**Interaction Philosophy:** Selections feel decisive — a single tap triggers a gold border highlight with a scale-up checkmark, then auto-advances after 400ms. The experience is linear and confident, like turning pages of a luxury lookbook.

**Animation:** Screens slide horizontally with a 500ms cubic-bezier ease. Elements fade in with 80ms stagger. The progress bar glides smoothly. No bounce, no overshoot — just controlled elegance.

**Typography System:** Playfair Display (600 weight) for headlines — classic, editorial, high-contrast serifs. Inter (300–500 weight) for body, labels, and buttons — clean, geometric, highly legible at small sizes. Generous letter-spacing on uppercase labels (0.08–0.15em).

## Idea 2: "Noir Luxe" — Inspired by Aman Resorts / Tom Ford

**Design Movement:** Dark luxury minimalism with cinematic depth

**Core Principles:**
1. Dark canvas as power — near-black backgrounds convey exclusivity
2. Gold as illumination — metallic accents glow against darkness
3. Cinematic framing — images feel like film stills
4. Whisper-quiet interactions — subtle, never loud

**Color Philosophy:** Deep charcoal (#141414) as the primary canvas, with warm off-white (#F5F0E8) text. Gold (#B8965A) used sparingly for active states and accents. The darkness creates a "private viewing room" atmosphere.

**Layout Paradigm:** Vertically stacked with generous breathing room. Cards arranged in a masonry-inspired asymmetric grid. Navigation floats at the bottom with frosted glass effect.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Frosted glass overlays on image cards
2. Gold-stroke borders that animate on selection
3. Grain texture overlay on the dark background

**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover reveals a subtle light sweep across cards. Selection triggers a gold border that draws itself clockwise. Everything feels like a private gallery experience.

**Animation:** Slow, deliberate fades (600ms). Cards enter with a slight upward drift. Progress bar pulses subtly with a gold glow.

**Typography System:** Cormorant Garamond for headlines — refined, high-contrast, old-world elegance. DM Sans for body — modern, clean, excellent readability on dark backgrounds.

## Idea 3: "Coastal Atelier" — Inspired by Soho House / Edition Hotels

**Design Movement:** Mediterranean coastal luxury meets contemporary art gallery

**Core Principles:**
1. Warm light as mood — everything bathed in golden-hour warmth
2. Organic geometry — soft curves, natural proportions
3. Tactile materiality — surfaces that suggest linen, stone, warm wood
4. Curated casualness — luxurious but never stiff

**Color Philosophy:** Sandy warm white (#FBF8F3) as canvas, deep olive (#3A3A2E) for text, terracotta (#C4856A) for accents, and warm brass (#B69D6E) for interactive states. The palette evokes a sunset over limestone architecture.

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric card layouts with varying sizes. The question headline sits left-aligned with generous left margin. Cards flow in an organic grid with slight size variations to create visual rhythm.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Rounded rectangle cards with 24px radius — like smooth river stones
2. Warm photographic overlays with a slight sepia tone
3. Handwritten-style decorative elements between sections

**Interaction Philosophy:** Cards have a gentle "lift" on hover — rising with a soft shadow. Selection adds a terracotta border with a satisfying tactile feel. Transitions feel like ocean waves — smooth, rhythmic, natural.

**Animation:** Organic easing curves (cubic-bezier 0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1). Cards breathe with subtle scale animations. Page transitions use a horizontal wipe effect.

**Typography System:** Lora for headlines — warm, readable serifs with a literary quality. Outfit for body — geometric sans-serif with friendly curves that complement the organic design language.

## Selected Approach: Idea 1 — "Editorial Minimalism"

This approach best matches the user's requirements for a Ritz-Carlton / Equinox feel with charcoal, cream, and muted gold. The editorial minimalism philosophy creates the high-end, no-clutter experience specified. Playfair Display + Inter is the exact font pairing requested (modern serif headline + clean sans-serif body). The warm cream background, decisive interactions, and cinematic image cards create a luxury brand interaction rather than a survey tool.
